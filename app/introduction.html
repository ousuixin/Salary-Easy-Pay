Salary Easy pay
  • This is a application to make salary pay easy
  • You can click the 'Employer Page' in the Home Page if you are a employer, then you can use it to create contract with your employee, pay salary to them, delete some contrct and so on.
  • You can click the 'Employee Page' in the Home Page if you are a employee, then you can use it to get salary from the contract give by your employer, see the deposit in the contract and so on.
